Our mission

Our mission is to facilitate community-led, people-centered, and sustainable outcomes with code, research, and design.

A critical mindset for all decisions that utilize technology and data.

A community-led and justice-driven approach for all design solutions.

A collaborative, organized, and enjoyable working process for every project.

What we do.

Web Development & Data Services

We build and maintain reliable, modern, and scalable full-stack web applications with tools like Ruby on Rails, Django, and React. We also build ways to analyze, manage, and beautifully visualize big datasets.

UX/UI Research & Design

Our approach to UX uses interviews, testing, and an iterative process that puts the needs, goals, and lived experiences of people first.

Digital Strategy & Technology Consulting

We advocate for responsible, grounded, and sustainable technology decisions that value empathy, justice, and collaboration. We advise on how to utilize technology in ways that minimize harm and maximize value.
