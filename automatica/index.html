Our mission

Our mission is to facilitate community-led, people-centered, and sustainable outcomes with code, research, and design.

We provide:

A critical approach towards all decisions surrounding technology and data.

A commitment to community-led and justice-driven design solutions.

A collaborative, organized, and enjoyable process in everything we do.

What we do

Web Development & Data Services

We architect and maintain reliable, modern, and scalable full-stack web applications with tools like Ruby on Rails, Django, and React. We also build ways to analyze, manage, and beautifully visualize big datasets.

UX/UI Research & Design

Our approach to UX uses interviews, testing, and an iterative process that puts the needs, goals, and lived experiences of people first.

Digital Strategy & Technology Consulting

We advocate for technology decisions grounded in empathy, justice, and accountability, as well as advise on how to use technology in ways that minimize harm and maximize value.
